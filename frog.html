Ramsey Canyon Leopard Frog

This frog species can be found in springs, streams, ponds, and lakes in Arizona and New Mexico.
They face endangerment primarily due to habitat loss from livestock grazing, dams, and water
diversions as well as water pollution and groundwater pumping.

  • color: olive/dark green/yellow, charcoal spots
  • diet: carnivorous
  • lifespan: 18 years
  • predators: bullfrogs, fish, crayfish
  • communication: vocalization, visual and physical signals
